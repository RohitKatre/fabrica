Products

SWAGED TUBULAR POLE

Manufactured from complete range of 410SP-1 to 410SP-80 (7 m to 16 m) according to standard specifications and customer requirements.

PIPE POLES

Our manufacturing range starts from 3m to 9 m and can be manufactured according to customer requirements. These poles are generally used for street lighting in township, commercial complexes, roads etc.

CAMERA POLES

Can be manufactured as per customer to technological requirement and are used for fixing all types of electrical to electronic devices

SMART POLES

It can be manufactured & designed as per customer & technical requirement and are used for fixing all types of electrical & electronics devices.

DISTRIBUTION POLES

Manufactured as per customer requirement & used for distribution of electricity.

SOLAR STREET POLES

Manufactured from range of 4.5 m to 13 m as per customer requirement for various solar light & panels.

INDUSTRIAL FABRICATION

We are also work in industrial fabrication such as industrial shed parts, industrial job work, and solar structure according to specification drawing provided by customer.

DECORATIVE BRACKETS

We also manufacture all types of decorative brackets (galvanized or on galvanized) as per customer requirement.

MANUFACTURING PROCESS

  • MS Sheet Cut to Length
  • Trapezoidal Cutting for octagonal or polygonal poles
  • Press Brake Bending
  • Longitudinal Welding Grinding
  • Base plate & door cutting & fitting
  • Galvanization
  • Straitening & Final Finishing
